EMERGENCY NUMBERS

DIAL 911 FOR ALL EMERGENCIES

For medical, fire, or police emergencies, always dial 911 first. Provide your exact address and nearest cross street. Blue Mountain addresses can be confusing for first responders, so be prepared with clear directions.

EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS TIPS

KEEP THIS INFORMATION HANDY

Post emergency numbers near all phones
Program important numbers into cell phones
Keep a printed copy in vehicles
Share with family members and guests
Update contact information annually

WHEN CALLING FOR HELP

PROVIDE CLEAR INFORMATION

  • Your exact address: House number and road name
  • Nearest cross street: Helps responders navigate
  • Landmarks: "Third house past the sharp curve"
  • Nature of emergency: Be specific about needs
  • Your phone number: In case of disconnection

Remember: Blue Mountain's winding roads can confuse GPS systems. Be prepared to give detailed directions to emergency responders.
