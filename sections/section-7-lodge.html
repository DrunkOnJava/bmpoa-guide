THE LODGE

The Blue Mountain Lodge serves as our community's central gathering place. This section provides information about the Lodge's facilities, rental procedures, and usage guidelines for all community members.

LODGE LOCATION & FEATURES

Address: 540 Cliff Road, Linden, VA 22642

The Blue Mountain Lodge is a community-owned facility that serves as the heart of our social activities and governance. Beautifully situated on Cliff Road with panoramic views of the valley, the Lodge provides an ideal setting for both formal meetings and casual gatherings.

FACILITY FEATURES

  • Main Hall: Accommodates up to 100 guests with flexible seating arrangements
  • Commercial Kitchen: Fully equipped with professional appliances, refrigerators, and preparation areas
  • Restrooms: ADA-compliant facilities for men and women
  • Lounge Area: Comfortable seating area with stone fireplace
  • Wraparound Deck: Outdoor space with mountain views, perfect for events
  • Parking: Ample parking for approximately 40 vehicles

AMENITIES INCLUDED

  • Tables and chairs for various configurations
  • Audio system with microphone
  • Projection screen for presentations
  • Wi-Fi internet access
  • Heating and air conditioning
  • Basic kitchen supplies and serving ware

REGULAR ACTIVITIES

The Lodge hosts numerous community activities throughout the year:

  • Monthly Board Meetings: Second Monday of each month at 6:00 PM EST
  • Social Committee Events: Quarterly gatherings and seasonal celebrations
  • Yoga Classes: Weekly sessions (check schedule on BMPOA website)
  • Private Events: Available for rental by property owners
  • Community Workshops: Educational sessions on various topics

ACCESSIBILITY

The Lodge is wheelchair accessible with ramp access to the main entrance and ADA-compliant restroom facilities. Please contact the Lodge Committee if you have specific accessibility needs for your event.

RENTAL INFORMATION & PRICING

The Blue Mountain Lodge is available for rental by property owners in good standing (current on Sanitary District taxes). The Lodge provides an excellent venue for family gatherings, celebrations, meetings, and other events.

RENTAL PROCESS

To reserve the Lodge for your event, follow these steps:

  1. Check availability by emailing bluemountainlodgebooking@gmail.com
  2. Provide the following information:
    • Your name and property address
    • Contact phone number and email
    • Requested date(s) and times
    • Type of event
    • Estimated number of attendees
  3. Review and sign the rental agreement
  4. Submit rental fee and security deposit
  5. Receive confirmation and access instructions

RENTAL RATES

CURRENT PRICING (Subject to Change)

  • Half Day (4 hours): $150 for property owners
  • Full Day (8 hours): $250 for property owners
  • Security Deposit: $200 (refundable)
  • Additional Hours: $40 per hour
  • Multi-day Events: Contact for special pricing

Note: Discounted rates available for BMPOA-sponsored events and non-profit organizations

INCLUDED IN RENTAL

  • Use of entire facility including kitchen and deck
  • Tables and chairs (setup assistance available)
  • Basic cleaning supplies
  • Utilities (electricity, water, heat/AC)
  • Parking area use
  • Wi-Fi access

NOT INCLUDED

  • Event setup and breakdown labor
  • Decorations or special equipment
  • Consumables (paper products, etc.)
  • Deep cleaning services

CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellations must be made at least 14 days in advance for a full refund. Cancellations within 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. No refunds for cancellations less than 7 days before the event, except for documented emergencies.

LODGE USAGE GUIDELINES

To ensure the Lodge remains a valuable community asset for all residents, please follow these usage guidelines. Your cooperation helps maintain this facility in excellent condition for everyone's enjoyment.

GENERAL RULES

  • The renter is responsible for the conduct of all guests
  • Maximum occupancy: 100 persons
  • No smoking inside the Lodge
  • Alcohol is permitted for private events (renter assumes all liability)
  • Music and noise must be kept at reasonable levels
  • All events must end by 10:00 PM (cleanup by 11:00 PM)
  • Children must be supervised at all times
  • Pets are not permitted inside the Lodge

SETUP & CLEANUP RESPONSIBILITIES

BEFORE LEAVING CHECKLIST

Remove all decorations and personal items
Clean all surfaces including tables, counters, and appliances
Sweep and mop floors (cleaning supplies provided)
Empty all trash and replace bags
Return furniture to original configuration
Clean kitchen thoroughly if used
Turn off all lights and appliances
Lock all doors and windows
Return keys as instructed

KITCHEN USE

The commercial kitchen is available for food preparation and service. Users must:

  • Clean all equipment and surfaces after use
  • Properly dispose of grease (never down drains)
  • Remove all food items when leaving
  • Report any equipment malfunctions immediately
  • Follow posted instructions for commercial appliances

DAMAGE & LIABILITY

IMPORTANT LIABILITY INFORMATION

  • Renters are liable for any damage beyond normal wear and tear
  • Security deposit will be withheld for damages or inadequate cleanup
  • Additional charges may apply for excessive cleaning or repairs
  • Renters must sign a liability waiver and provide proof of homeowner's insurance
  • BMPOA is not responsible for personal property or injuries during private events

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

In case of emergency during your event:

  1. Call 911 for medical, fire, or police emergencies
  2. Contact the Lodge Committee Chair for facility issues
  3. Emergency exits are clearly marked - ensure guests know locations
  4. First aid kit located in kitchen cabinet
  5. Fire extinguisher locations: kitchen and main hall
